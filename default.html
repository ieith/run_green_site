Seeking a More Environmental Way to Run


**Please note that this website is coded wholly by myself, using HTML and CSS. As such, the styling and layout is still very early days!!! All photos are taken by myself and are my direct property, unless otherwise stated.


Intro

As time has gone on, I have found myself increasingly inclined towards minimising my environmental impact. Unfortunately, I am also a runner. This means trainers, tshirts, transportation, technical doodads and all the other bumf and paraphernalia that comes with it. Or does it? Do we need all this stuff? Does being a contemporary runner mean being a consumerist runner? Do I need to buy 3 pairs of polarised lenses and change my shoes every 500 miles or whatever? This website is my attempt to answer that question.

About me
I am middle-aged, overweight, vegetarian, a run leader, a trail leader and a member of The Green Runners. I run long distances not very fast, usually with a decent amount of elevation. My running is not necessarily "running".
About this site
This sites serves a threefold purpose:
  • Firstly, it is to document my exploration in increasing my sustainability as a runner and serving as a resource for those on the same journey (sharing knowledge is a key piece in my personal code);
  • Secondly, as part of my Green Runner's pledge I vowed to be overt in flying the flag (which I am doing through this site)
  • Finally, it allows me to work on my web development skills in a way that actually means something to me.
